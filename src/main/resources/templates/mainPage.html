Rooms
  • Double Room

    Our double rooms are 20m2 and have twin beds or french beds. At least 1 adult and at the most 2 + 1 adults could accommodate in these rooms.

  • Triple Room

    Our triple rooms are 23m2 and 33 m2. Each room has one french bed and twin bed. 3 adults could accommodate in these rooms.

  • Deluxe Room

    Our deluxe room is 40m2 and have a french bed. At least 1 adult and at the most 5 adults could accommodate in this room
