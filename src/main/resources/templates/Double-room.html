Double room

Room Description

Our double rooms are 20m2 and have twin beds or french beds. At least 1 adult and at the most 2 + 1 adults could accommodate in these rooms.

These rooms are ceramic-floored and have split-air conditioner, 82cm Led TV, bathroom and WC, internet access (wi-fi), safety deposit box, hair drier, mini-bar, direct telephone and balcony.
